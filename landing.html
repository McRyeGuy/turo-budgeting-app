Dashboard

Fleet net worth, total earnings, expenses, profit, and ROI. Stacked-by-car earnings chart you can flip between views.

Per-car deep dive

Click any car for utilization, ADR, breakeven, payback period, depreciation projections, and a monthly chart.

Smart expenses

One-off or recurring. Apply to one car or split across many — perfect for shop visits or insurance.

VIN auto-decode

Drop your CSV and we decode the VIN via NHTSA — year, make, model, trim. Mileage pulled from check-in odometer.

Loans & financing

Track loan terms per car. Payments auto-flow into expenses; outstanding balance reduces net worth.

Mileage-aware values

Current values use a tiered depreciation curve adjusted for actual miles — plus a KBB lookup link for ground truth.

Photo library

Upload a photo for each car. Shows on dashboard cards and in the detail view so you can ID them at a glance.

Cross-device sync

Log in from your phone, laptop, or tablet. Your data is encrypted and synced automatically through your account.

100% private

Your data lives in your encrypted account. Export a backup anytime. No selling data, no ads, ever.
