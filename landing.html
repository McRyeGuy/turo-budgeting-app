Your data, your privacy

We built this tool for ourselves first — so trust matters to us too.

Who can see my earnings data?

Only you. Your data is encrypted in your personal account and never shared, sold, or accessed by us.

Is my Turo account safe?

We never ask for your Turo login. You export a CSV from Turo and upload it here — we never connect directly to Turo.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Delete any vehicle, expense, or your entire account at any time. Your data is purged immediately.
