Total earnings minus tolls and gas reimbursements

Why is this different from my Turo earnings tab? ▾ Hide explanation ▴

Turo's earnings tab shows everything that hit your account, including reimbursements for things you already paid out of pocket. This tracker subtracts those out so the number reflects what you actually kept.

Tolls & tickets and gas reimbursements are not income — they're guests paying you back for tolls, parking tickets, or gas they used. That money has to go right back out to cover the bill, so counting it as earnings would inflate your profit.

Formula used here:

earnings = Total earnings − Tolls & tickets − Gas reimbursement  + ($10 × number of gas reimbursements)

Optional $10 fuel fee: when the toggle in Settings is on, $10 is added back per trip with a gas reimbursement — representing your fuel service fee on top of what the gas actually cost. Toggle it off in Settings to remove this.
